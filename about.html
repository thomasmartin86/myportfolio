Bio

Originally from South Florida, I've been living in Orlando since 2005. Growing up, I was always drawn towards computers and technology and began programming in high school. I studied Information Technology at the University of Central Florida and have been in the technology industry ever since. I've worked as a developer and systems administrator, both as part of a team, and as a solo freelancer. I love learning about new technologies and am consistently looking for ways to improve my skillset. When I'm not working or coding you'll find me spending time with my family and exploring the outdoors.
